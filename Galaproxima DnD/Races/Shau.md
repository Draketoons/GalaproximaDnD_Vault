[[Races]]
(Goat People)

Home World: Gatra, Andromeda Galaxy

Details:  
	The Shau are a bipedal mammalian goat-like race from the planet Gatra, a gas giant world scattered with floating islands covered in gigantic mushrooms. They are known for their tall stature, spiraling horns, and gigantic fluffy ears. Their home world’s fungi emit special spores that the shau constantly breath, giving them a constant pleasant high, but if they were to ever breath air that’s not full of these spores, they will experience withdrawal symptoms so severe that it’s fatal, and they will die within the hour. Due to the symbiotic connection with the fungi, the shau can telepathically communicate with each other.

**Culture/Politics:**
	Due to their constant intoxication, they have achieved world peace ever since their civilization’s conception, basically an entire planet of hippies, this also influences them to be a very spiritual race as they worship the fungi that gave them their way of life. Their customs discourage the use of excessive clothing, so the most the average shau will wear is something akin to a poncho or some sort of shawl, but most prefer to be nude. There’s a certain minority of shau that have developed a tolerance to the fungi’s effect and because of this their sense of identity and ideals don’t exactly align with the norms of Gatra, so most of those shau leave their home world in search of a new life throughout the galaxy. These special shau also developed space travel, as the rest of their race didn’t exactly have an interest in it.

**Abilities:**

Glide:
	If a Shau is falling, they can lift their large droopy ears up and begin gliding as the air fills their ears, just like on their home world. The Shau will not take any fall damage once they reach the ground.

Passive:
	A shau needs to breathe in the spores of a gatradian fungus in order to stay alive, otherwise they will experience withdrawal symptoms and then die within 20 minutes. All rolls are at a disadvantage while the character is without the fungus.
