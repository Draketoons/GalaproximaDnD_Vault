[[Races]]
(Magma blob people)

Home World: Malo, Andromeda Galaxy

**Details:**
	The Qoro are a race of large sentient magma blobs from the planet Malo, a lava world scattered with erupting volcanoes and beautiful towering crystal pillars. Their bodies are mostly rock, but they have a special organ that superheats their bodies to a malleable state which allows them to move and shape their bodies. Each qoro has a unique shape which they can choose to align with their personal identity, there are some identifying features that each qoro has however. They all have some sort of identifiable head shape which holds their brain, as well as two big buds on the head which hold sensory organs that can sense the environment around them through vibrations. Some qoro have certain features like crystals protruding from their bodies depending on the minerals that make up the body. Qoro reproduce asexually, as new qoro are formed from the magma that spews from a special volcano on their home world 

**Culture/Politics:**
	The Qoro greatly value scientific progress and development, which makes them very utilitarian in nature. They can come off as cold and indifferent but they really don't mean anything by it as they make decisions based off of what they consider to be the greater good. The qoro consider most feelings like love or attachment to be irrational, and believe that pure logical and rational thought is the best way forward, so for any qoro that develop these kinds of emotional reactions they get 'reeducated' and are discouraged to give into these kinds of mushy feelings. The qoro are exceptional at developing new kinds of materials for various applications, so their home world, Malo, is a material manufacturing hub for the galaxy.

**Abilities:**

Form Shift:
	A Qoro can change their general shape into a couple of useful forms:
		Wall Form: 
		A qoro can morph into a wall 10-20 ft wide, which blocks incoming attacks and projectiles, but the Qoro themself will still take damage from those attacks
		Small Form: 
		A qoro can compress their bodies into a smaller form, so the qoro is now considered a small creature.
		Large Form: 
		A qoro can expand their bodies into a larger form, so the qoro is now considered a large creature.

**Passive:**
	Anything flammable that comes in contact with a qoro’s skin will burst into flames
	Any physical attacks by a Qoro will inflict burning damage on the target
	Qoro are resistant to burning damage but weak to cold
