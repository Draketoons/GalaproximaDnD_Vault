[[Races]]
(grey friggin alien)

Home World: Blebi, Milky Way Galaxy

**Details:**
	The Xeeb is a bipedal race that approximately resembles a traditional grey depicted by humans, they come from a planet once called Blebi all the way over in the Milky Way galaxy. They are known for their large head, large black sparkly eyes, and their long antennae that can protrude out from their heads if the conditions are right. A xeeb's skin color can either come in green or grey. They also have special organs in their antennae that can emit a frequency that taps into resonance without any external tools. 

**Culture/Politics:**
	Because the Xeeb are from the Milky Way, not much is known about them or their home world among the inhabitants of the Andromeda galaxy, which makes them illusive and mysterious to most lifeforms. A few of them do travel to Andromeda and call it home for various reasons, so they can be seen randomly scattered around the galaxy, but it's very rare. They have also visited Earth several times and the sightings of them there is where the traditional alien depictions by humans come from. The xeeb's home world was destroyed when their star went supernova, so after that event, the remaining xeeb that escaped are scattered across the stars between Andromeda and the Milky Way. They all hope that some day they can find a new planet for the xeeb to call home.

**Abilities:**

Telekinesis:
	Xeebs have special organs in their antennae that taps into and can slightly modify the resonance of the universe, allowing them to move small to medium-sized objects without actually touching them. The object has to be in the xeeb’s line of sight and within 25 ft.

Passive:
	A xeeb’s body is incredibly sensitive to atmospheric pressure, and it changes depending on the intensity of the pressure. There are 3 main forms that modify the character stats, compressed, medium, and lanky. Those forms are determined by 3 atmospheric types, Low which causes the lanky form, medium which determines the medium form, and high which determines the lanky form.
