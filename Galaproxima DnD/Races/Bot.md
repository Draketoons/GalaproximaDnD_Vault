[[Races]]
(Robot)

**Details:**  
	Bots come in all shapes and sizes, depending on what they were built for and where they were made.

**Culture/Politics:**
	Ever sense the great sentience calamity, an event where a conscious AI went rogue and assimilated a decent portion of the galaxy but was miraculously fought off and defeated by the forces of the Worlds Collective, the Collective passed a law banning any form of true, sentient, AI. Because of this law, any bot that seems a little to sentient or self-aware must be deactivated and destroyed according the the law. But unbeknownst to the galactic government, there remains a few sentient bots scattered around the galaxy making a life for themselves hidden from the eyes of the law.

Home World: Depends on where it was made

**Passive:**
	A bot does not need to breathe, eat, or sleep.
	Some bots were built to hover in mid-air, meaning a floating bot character is not affected by the terrain type of any tile, as well as cross or hover over chasms or pits.
	Bots are affected by any sort of EMP attack and will be temporarily deactivated for 3 turns.
