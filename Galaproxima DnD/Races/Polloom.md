[[Races]]
(Plant People)

Home World: Moyli, moon of Ipryl, Andromeda Galaxy

**Details:**
	The pollom are a botanical race of sapient plants from Moyli, a moon orbiting the gas giant Ipryl. They are known for their big flower-like heads with beady eyes attached to long flexible stems protruding out from the flower. Their bodies are essentially just 4 long vines that they can control which grows longer as they mature.

**Culture/Politics:**
	Pollooms greatly value nature and ecological balance, in fact all of their technology is grown rather than manufactured. In other words all their tech is essentially just highly specialized and complex plants, which includes their spaceships, weapons, etc. They developed this way because developing technology any other way could harm their planet and disrupt their way of life. Pollooms strongly believe in their way of advancement and spacefaring and are highly critical of all the other races who have developed tech the more straightforward way, doing ecological harm to their home worlds.

**Abilities:** 

Vine Whip:
	A Polloom can use their vine limbs to whip any creature within 5-10 ft causing 1d4 slashing damage.

Passive:
1. If any sort of flame gets in contact with a polloom they will immediately burst into flames due to their plant-like bodies being incredibly flammable. A polloom on fire will take 1d4 burning damage per turn.
2. While pollooms have the ability to eat food, they also have the ability to photosynthesize instead if they're close enough to a yellow main-sequence star. 
3. Pollooms are resistant to poison damage but weak to burning
