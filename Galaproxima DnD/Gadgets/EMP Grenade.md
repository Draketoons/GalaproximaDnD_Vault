A simple grenade that emits a powerful electro magnetic pulse. This grenade does not damage organic life-forms, but can damage or deactivate electronic devices or stun cyforms. It takes an action to throw it and once it hits something, anything electronic, including things like weapons, within a 10 ft radius is deactivated. If the units within range are cyforms, they must take 1d8 lighting damage and must succeed in a constitution saving throw or be stunned for the next 2 turns.
