[[Gadgets]]
A small flying drone capable of projecting a shield onto a small to medium sized unit. During battle the user can pick a unit they see in the area and make strength check to throw the drone at the unit, if they succeed the drone makes it instantly and generates the shield on the selected unit. If the user fails in the strength check, the drone makes it to the selected unit on the user's next turn. Once the shield is generated, that unit has their AC multiplied by 1.5 rounded to the highest number for the rest of the battle until the unit is attacked, once the unit is attacked the shield is broken.

**Recipe**
	1 Microchip
	3 Battery
	2 Metal Plating
