[[Gadgets]]
A simple frag grenade, takes an action to throw it from a 5 to 20 ft radius. Once the grenade makes contact with something, anything within a 5 ft radius immediately takes 1d8 piercing damage and any unit that is at 10 ft away must succeed in a dexterity saving throw or take 1d6 piercing damage.

**Recipe:**
	1 Battery
	2 Metal Plating
