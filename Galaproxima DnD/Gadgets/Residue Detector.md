[[Gadgets]]
A small cube shaped gadget that can be thrown or placed on a surface, and detect and playback previous life-form's actions within a 10 ft radius. The holographic playback itself is pretty blurry and only shows vague silhouettes of the life-forms that were there previously. It takes a lot of energy to display this and conduct the algorithms so it only lasts about a minute and after that it needs to recharge.

**Recipe:**
	2 Metal Plating
	2 Battery
	1 Microchip
	2 Copper Wiring
