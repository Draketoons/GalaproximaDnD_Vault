Each ship has these basic features:

**Hull:**
	A ship's hull is what shields the inside from the outside of the ship and it has hit points to, once a ship's hull reaches zero the ship is considered destroyed.
	A ship's hull also has an AC which is determined by the type of ship

**Gadget nodes:**
	Ships have nodes on various parts of the ship in which weapons or tools can be placed on them.

**Engines:**
	Every ship has an engine, which determines movement speed. 
	Engines have hit points to and can be targeted and hit during battle, the lower the engine hp, the slower the speed.
