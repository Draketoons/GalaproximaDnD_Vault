[[Songs]]
An offensive song in which the performer creates a small ball of electricity 5 ft in front of the performer, the performer can move the spark around with the same movement speed as the performer. Any adjacent units within a 5 ft radius at the end of the movement must make a dexterity saving throw or take 1d4 lightning damage. The ball's movement replaces the performer's movement, so the performer cannot move while the ball is active. The ball will disappear when the performer decides or if the performer takes any form of damage.

**Level:** 1
**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Indefinite
**Duration:** Indefinite
