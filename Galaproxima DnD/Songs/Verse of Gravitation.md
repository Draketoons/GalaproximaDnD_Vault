A simple verse which allows the performer to pick any small to medium sized object they can see within 80 ft and move it as they please by manipulating small pockets of gravity. This verse can either be used as a utility by moving objects, or an attack by accelerating an object towards a target. If the performer decides to use it as an attack the target must succeed in a dexterity check against the performers constitution. if the target fails, they will take bludgeoning damage dependent on the type of object thrown. 

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 80 ft
**Duration:** Indefinite
