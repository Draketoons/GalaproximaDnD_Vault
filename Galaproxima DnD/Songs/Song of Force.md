[[Songs]]
A simple offensive song in which the performer chooses a row in any cardinal direction, any units within this row must make a strength saving throw or be pushed back 15 ft and take 1d4 thunder damage. If a unit is pushed up against an object or wall they must make a dexterity saving throw or take 1d6 bludgeoning damage.

**Level:** 1
**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 15 ft
**Duration:** Instantaneous
