If the players choose the milk run, they will receive a digital contract and must head to Gatra.
upon entering the Gatrian system, they will head to Gatra's orbit to get to Gatra's main trading station

*Orbiting the cloudy giant is a quaint but somewhat bustling space station, this is the main trading platform for all of Gatra's exports*

Upon arriving at the landing bay, the players will eventually find a burly shau named Norl if the players present the contract he will direct the station to load up the ship and send the players on their way

*"A contract? OH you guys are the milk-runners! should be a simple enough job, just make sure you get this stuff to Crolum station within 6 arc-days, otherwise it'll start to spoil.. Oh and also you'll be compensated for fuel"*

*The shau then presses on his ear pieces and shouts "ALRIGHT we need 8 creates of spore milk down in bay 4!" as he begins walking away*

After the ship is all loaded up, the players will then leave the station to start their cargo run
