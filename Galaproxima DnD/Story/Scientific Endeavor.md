If the players choose the scientific mission, they will receive a digital contract and must head to a specific material research lab on Malo.

*As soon as you pass through the upper layer Malo's thick atmosphere, almost overheating on entry, the red sun's dim light immediately becomes even dimmer as the only thing properly lighting this hostile planet surface is the orange glow of magma rivers and lightning strikes. But you couldn't help but notice the beautiful enormous crystalline pillars that break through the ground and touch the sky.*

The players then follow the coordinates and arrive at the material research lab

*Suddenly in the fast landscape of active volcanoes, you see a somewhat large industrial like building suspended over a magma river between two tall cliffs with pipes leading down below.*

*Luckily there seems to be a landing bay, but you have to request for a landing*

The players can request a landing, upon the request a qoro asks why the players are requesting landing, if they explain the contract the qoro will let them land.

*"What is your purpose here. this area is for researches only"*

*"Oh, I see, you are the life-forms that took our job-posting, you proceed to landing bay 01"*

*The chaotic sounds of the outside hellscape are finally muffled as the bay doors close while landing*

After waiting for a while, the players are indifferently greeted by a qoro named Olpo.

*"Greetings. we here are very appreciative of all of you taking part in this experiment"*

*Before much else can be said, 2 more qoros enter, carrying an industrial looking hermetically sealed box, with a glass window on top to what's inside, which appears to be some sort of a thick purple liquid, as well a computer terminal on the side.*

*"We have discovered a strange new material from a recent meteor impact not too far away from this facility. Which is strange because typically meteors just burn up in our atmosphere due to it's intense pressure but that's beside the point. We need you to test out a theory, we believe exposing this material to an immensely strong magnetic field could activate certain properties that can't disclose"*

*The 2 other qoros begin to load this box onto the ship*

"We want you to travel to the nearest neutron star, which we have provided coordinates for, and try to get as close as possible without damaging the ship and see what happens, then log the data on the terminal and come back, should be simple enough."

If the players accept Olpo will wish them luck and send them on their way.
