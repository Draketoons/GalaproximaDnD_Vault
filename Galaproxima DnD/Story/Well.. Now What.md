After the space battle, either by defeating the Consordium ships or by escaping through an emergency tunnel jump, the players are finally safe and can interact and figure out what to do next.

One of the options is to take an odd-job found on the galactic merc-board, accessed on the ship computer.

The jobs currently available are listed on the monitor
	>*Need 8 crates of spore milk from Gatra. please deliver to Crolum station. // Will transfer 100 bits upon delivery
	>Need pilots for scientific mission, head to this material research lab on Malo, will give more details upon arrival. // 200 bits.

The players will get to choose what job to take, which will determine what happens next...
