1 = Law enforcement encounter:
	The players encounter a police ship, the ship hails and orders a manifest of the player's cargo, if the player has anything illegal the officers will threaten and demand the players hand over the cargo and pay a hefty fee or be arrested, if the players refuse they enter battle. if the players escape the battle, they are now wanted.

9 = Asteroid field
	The players encounter a large asteroid field, the pilot will have to make 3 piloting checks of DC 12 to clear it, each time the pilot fails the piloting check the ship's hull must take 1d4 damage.
