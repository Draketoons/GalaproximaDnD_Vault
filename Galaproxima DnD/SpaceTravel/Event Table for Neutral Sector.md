1 = Pirate encounter
	The players encounter a pirate ship, the pirate will hail the player's ship and demand 150 bits and a manifest of their cargo, if the players refuse to negotiate they will enter combat with the pirate.

9 = Asteroid field
	The players encounter a large asteroid field, the pilot will have to make 3 piloting checks of DC 12 to clear it, each time the pilot fails the piloting check the ship's hull must take 1d4 damage.

5 = Roaming Merchant
	The players encounter a Traveling merchant ship, the merchant hail the player's ship and offer 1d6 trades
