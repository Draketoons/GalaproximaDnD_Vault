1 = Consordium Encounter
	The players encounter 1d4 Consordium star ships, one of the ships will hail the player's ship and demand they identify themselves. If the players refuse they enter battle, if the players comply and none of the players are lynexian they will enter battle.

4 = Polloom refugee
	The players encounter a seemingly still and silent consordium ship, upon further inspection there is a polloom prisoner aboard, the polloom was able to overpower and defeat the soldiers aboard. If the players choose to help the polloom will board their ship and will be dropped off at the nearest planet or space station outside of the consordium sectors.

9 = Asteroid field
	The players encounter a large asteroid field, the pilot will have to make 3 piloting checks of DC 12 to clear it, each time the pilot fails the piloting check the ship's hull must take 1d4 damage.
