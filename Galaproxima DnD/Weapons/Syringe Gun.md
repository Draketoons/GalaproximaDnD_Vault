[[Weapons]]
The syringe gun is a tool/weapon typically used by Xeno-Biologists, like the name implies it allows the user to shoot syringes in order to inflict bio hacks onto targets from afar. Shooting a syringe counts as an action and the gun has a range of 40 ft. It also needs to be reloaded with a syringe which also counts as an action.

**Weapon Type:** Special
**Range:** 40 ft
